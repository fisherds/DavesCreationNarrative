Era 0 - The Big Bang and Boundry of the Void

Before time began there was nothing, no laws of physics, no dimensions, no time, only one God, and it was not good. So God set about the business of designing, building, sustaining, and maintaining life. God began to go forth from God.

Era 1 - Spiritual Space

The heavenly abode: As God went out from God. Non-physical space (spiritual) was made and God had gone forth from God, but no created beings filled the spiritual space to share the non-physical creation.

Important side note about the pre-creation space, i.e. nobody's space. As God went out from God, the void was not destroyed, simply divided from spiritual space.

Era 2 - Physical Space

The physical cosmos abode: As God went out from God. Physical space began not as a cosmic boom, but as a natural expansion of God, moving forward at the speed of light as the created cosmos pushed into the void of nothing. Dimensional space, following the ordering principles of time, space, matter, and physical laws moved forwards at the speed of light dividing the void. And God reflected on his work and it had the potential for sustainable life.

Era 1b - Filling Spiritual Space

Since we can't really perceive Non-physical beings it's best to look at the sun, moon, stars, and other planets of our solar system and image them as symbols representing spiritual beings that God created to fill the spiritual space. The hosts of spiritual space are given authority to rule the spiritual space, and their symbols provide light, time markers, and order in the physical space.

Era 2b - Life on Earth

Next we take a rather special interest in one planet. God goes forth from God and creates life on Earth. The grasses grass. The fruit trees fruit. And God saw that it was good.

Era 3 - Designing Nephesh Hiya נֶפֶשׁ חַיָּה

Beings with the Breath of Life in Them

Opportunities 🚀

  • Beings need to be free to choose for themselves how to exist
  • Love can grow.

Threats ⚠️

  • Free beings may not love you back
  • A free being can cause non-life and threaten the life to other free living beings

Let's start with beings that can multiple according to their kind that lack the mental capacity / potential to be images of God. We also need to set limits on how physical and non-physical beings communicate. We also need to invite to the images of God to receive a revelation of God. Perhaps this will take more than one story.

Era 3a - Animals

Animals need to fill all the spaces of the earth. They are free. They are good. They are not tame.

Era 3b - Humans

This is going to take some time and will take some significant ongoing effort to make a being capable of partnership with God. These beings will be eternal and live in the unified physical and non-physical space, but perhaps not all at once. God saw what he had made so far and it was very good.

Currrent Era - The Already and the Not Yet

Right now life as moved very far into God's plan. God has gone forth into the Spiritual space as the Holy Spirit and God has gone forth into the Physical space as Jesus. We find ourselves living in the already and the not-yet of God's grand plan. And the story continues...
